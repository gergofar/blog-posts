I decided to start this space simply to share the interesting things I run into.

In my day-to-day work leading engineering teams and driving AI adoption, I often come across challenges, solutions, or ideas that are worth a deeper look. But my interest in this field doesn't stop when I finish my workday. I spend a lot of my free time building machine learning models from scratch and tinkering with new concepts, so you can expect findings from those personal deep dives to pop up here as well.

Going forward, whenever I stumble upon something fascinating—whether it's a practical reality of applied AI, the mechanics of training models, a reflection on engineering leadership, or an interesting concept from my math studies - I'll write a post about it.

There is no grand content strategy or strict publishing schedule. Expect a mix of technical thoughts, leadership lessons, and real-world engineering experiences, shared as they happen.

Thanks for stopping by.
